Your 15 minutes singing practice of the day

1

Pick a challenge

A song you like, a singer that inspires you. Find inspiration from the featured performances of talented artists at Flowra or add any performance you like from YouTube.

Focus on a small part

Don't practice a song from start to end. Instead focus on one small part like the Chorus or even just a line from the Chorus. Flowra lets you click on a line in the lyrics to select the part of the song you'd like to practice.
